American Red Cross

Flat * 2006

Five years after the devastating 9/11 attacks, America was still steeped in the aftereffects of the catastrophic events: residual medical ailments, missing family members, and PTSD were just a few of the persistent problems. The American Red Cross had built the 9/11 Legacy Fund to provide assistance for these problems, however most of the public was unaware of what purpose, and to what extent, it served.

Bowie & Co

2014

What do economics, artists, gutterpunks, and river rats have in common? Nothing. However, everyone needs to pay taxes, and to get a business loan, you need accounting. With Katie Bowie, owner of Bowie & Co, I provided a rebranding of her identity, copywriting, and an entirely new online presence for their creative, unique, and arts-industry-focused approach to providing accounting and tax consulting to all.
